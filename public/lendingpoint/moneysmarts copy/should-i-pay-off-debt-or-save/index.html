Should I Pay Off Debt or Save?

It can be difficult to know whether you should pay off debt before saving. Fortunately, you can do both. Answer some quick questions to help you prioritize.

Many people struggle to determine whether they should prioritize paying off debt or build up their savings for one simple reason: the answer depends on your individual circumstances and goals.


The good news is you don’t need to choose one or the other. Find the balance right for you by answering a few quick questions about your financial situation.
